## Astoria

Astoria is a neighborhood in the western portion of the New York City borough of Queens. Astoria is bounded by the East River and is adjacent to four other Queens neighborhoods: Long Island City to the southwest, Sunnyside to the southeast, and Woodside and East Elmhurst to the east. As of 2019, Astoria has an estimated population of 154,141.

---

### Key Details
- **WHAT TO EXPECT:** A dense, diverse western Queens neighborhood along the East River, with a mix of historic sections like Old Astoria, subsections such as Ravenswood and Ditmars, and active commercial corridors.


- **UNEXPECTED APPEAL:** A long history as a filmmaking and industrial hub, with Kaufman Astoria Studios and the Museum of the Moving Image preserving its early cinema legacy.


- **THE MARKET:** Predominantly multifamily housing in a high-density urban setting, with population spread across Astoria, Old Astoria, Steinway, and other subsections.



---

### Around the Block

Astoria is a western Queens neighborhood bounded by the East River and defined by its high density and notable ethnic diversity. It includes Old Astoria, Ravenswood, Ditmars, and Astoria Heights, and forms part of Queens Community District 1.
